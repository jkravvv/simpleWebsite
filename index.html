Personal Values

Honesty

Honesty means being real and telling the truth, even when it’s not easy.

This comes from how I was raised and the importance of trust in my life.

It matters because it helps build real relationships.

Without it, people lose trust and connections can fall apart.

Respect

Respect is treating people how you’d want to be treated.

This comes from family, school, and everyday interactions.

It’s important because it keeps things positive and avoids conflict.

Without respect, relationships can become negative and stressful.

Communication

Communication is about expressing yourself clearly and listening to others.

This comes from working with others in different situations.

It’s important because it prevents misunderstandings.

Without it, problems don’t get solved and confusion builds up.

Confidence

Confidence means believing in yourself and taking chances.

This comes from personal growth and stepping outside my comfort zone.

It’s important because it helps me go after opportunities.

Without it, I might hold myself back and miss out on growth.
